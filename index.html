The Four Actions Framework

Reconstructing Market Boundaries

Eliminate

  • Direct Healthcare Provision: Avoids costs/liabilities by being a facilitator.
  • Physical Infrastructure: Eliminates need for brick-and-mortar locations.
  • Broad Service Competition: No longer competes with general directories.

Reduce

  • General-Purpose Focus: Narrows scope to maternal care, reducing complexity.
  • User Search Effort: Unified platform and smart matching saves user time.
  • Reliance on Unverified Reviews: Emphasizes credential verification for trust.

Raise

  • Provider Vetting & Credibility: Multi-layered verification raises trust above the standard.
  • Specialization & Niche Focus: Becomes the go-to expert resource.
  • Convenience & Ease of Access: Consolidates all services in one integrated app.

Create

  • Unified Maternal Care Ecosystem: A single platform for all relevant services.
  • Intelligent, Need-Based Matching: Algorithm for personalized connections.
  • Seamless Integration with Tracker App: Creates a powerful, interconnected user experience.
